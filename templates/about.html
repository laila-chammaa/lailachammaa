    about Laila



    Hi guys! My name is Laila, as you can probably tell by now since it's written all over this website.. sorry about that. That's me on the left. I used an old picture cause I don't look that cute anymore.

    I’m a programmer with a love for tiramisu, bookstores, playlists, montreal, bts, and lychee drinks.

    When I first started thinking about what I wanted to major in, I was looking through a list of the top-paying majors (as you do), and I kept seeing "computer science" and when I asked my family what that was, they'd all answer: it's too complicated, something to do with engineering, it's a guy's thing. And that's it. That's all I needed to know to get me interested. A new challenge.

    For the past 2 years, I’ve been studying different programming concepts and languages (including python, java, html, css, flask, and javascript.. obviously) at concordia and online (on platforms like brilliant.org, codecademy, and Datacamp) to get me closer to my goal of working with a culturally diverse team in a plant filled office with open windows and lots of sun... yep that's all i'm getting my degree for. I'm currently in my second year of university.

    I believe in wide curiosity, “good trouble”, and ending up where you’re not supposed to be. Everyone is a fellow traveller.
